Easy Ipsum

Easy Ipsum is a quick and simple way to get placeholder "lorem ipsum" text onto your clipboard.

This site only works on browser's with adobe flash compatibility. I used the frameworks loremjs: https://github.com/f/loremjs and zeroclipboard https://github.com/zeroclipboard/zeroclipboard. This little project was inspired by the mac app LittleIpsum. I recommend checking it out if you want a software solution for quick lorem ipsum.
